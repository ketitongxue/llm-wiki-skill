# Purpose

## Mission

Build a connected knowledge base about a clearly defined domain so that durable explanations can be reused across future questions and sources.

## Audience

People and Agents who need traceable, maintainable knowledge rather than isolated summaries.

## In scope

- Mechanisms, concepts, entities, and decisions that serve the mission.
- Source records needed to verify provenance.
- Hubs and indexes needed to navigate the knowledge.

## Out of scope

- Unrelated personal records.
- Credentials and private configuration.
- Source copying that is not required for lawful provenance or analysis.

## Quality bar

Every durable page must be understandable without its original ingestion session, link to related knowledge, and distinguish sourced claims from synthesis.
